# 2. Related Work

## 2.1 LLMs for Clinical NLP and Psychiatric Diagnosis

- General medical LLM benchmarks (MedQA, PubMedQA, etc.)
- Psychiatric-specific applications: depression detection, suicide risk assessment
- Limitations: hallucination, lack of structured reasoning, poor calibration

## 2.2 Multi-Agent Systems for Medical Reasoning

- Agent-based approaches to clinical decision support
- Role decomposition in medical AI (specialist, generalist, judge)
- Debate and ensemble methods for diagnostic accuracy

## 2.3 Chinese Clinical NLP and Somatization

- Chinese medical NLP: named entity recognition, relation extraction
- Somatization in Chinese psychiatry: cultural context and clinical significance
- Cross-cultural diagnostic challenges in ICD-10/DSM-5 application

## 2.4 Evidence-Grounded Diagnosis Systems

- Retrieval-augmented generation for clinical applications
- Criterion-based diagnostic systems
- Confidence calibration and abstention in medical AI
